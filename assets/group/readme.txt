Drop group photos here to replace the auto-generated placeholders.
Name them 1.png, 2.jpg, 3.webp, ... — PNG, JPG, and WEBP all work, and you
can mix formats freely. The app shuffles randomly between however many it
finds, never showing the same one twice in a row (if there's just one, it
stays put). Missing files are fine; the app falls back to a clean
placeholder.

Landscape shots (e.g. 1200x800) look best, but any shape works — the WHOLE
photo is always shown, shrunk to fit if needed, never stretched or cut off.
